Our Team

Our Team

  • Larch

    Co-founder. Dr. Larch Maxey LLB, MSc, PhD, FRGS. Has researched, written about & co-created pioneering sustainable communities since 1994, including the pioneering Lammas eco-village; national charity the Network of Wellbeing & 2 internationally renowned research groups. Larch initiated Wales' One Planet Development Policy, was chair of the Ecological Land Cooperative & Director of Bright Green Futures, the community self-build enterprise responsible for Bristol's most sustainable homes. Larch has co-edited 2 books & is a devoted father & Short Moments practitioner, enthusiastic surfer & lifelong gardener.

  • Steffie

    Director. Dr. Steffie Broer BSc, MSc, PhD, founded Bright Green Futures after 8 years advising Sustainable Energy Solutions at ESD (now Verco) and completing a Masters (Distinction) Applied Energy & Buildings (Cranfield University) and a Doctorate in Engineering at UCL’s Centre for Urban Sustainability and Resilience. Steffie won an award for her publication on how to achieve low carbon livelihoods in new build housing communities and the South West Energy Efficiency Champion of the Year Award.

  • Andrew

    Architectural Advisor. At the age of 40, combining interests in art, engineering, music and design, Andrew qualified as an architect and joined the Riba as a member. Andrew worked in two practices before starting his own, where his projects were mainly domestic, including loft conversions, extensions and new builds. Along the way, Andrew developed an interest in, and explored extensively, sthapatya ved - the vedic tradition of architecture based entirely on working with nature. Now retired, Andrew maintains an interest in using design to create housing that is appealing, sustainable and cost-effective.

  • Tony

    Co-founder. I'm Tony, living in Scotland but originally from London and often there, a retired telecommunications and electrical engineer. I am a seasoned climate campaigner and have been concerned about the climate and environment since I was a child. I am a keen student of the work of climate scientists Bill McGuire, Peter Kalmus, Peter Carter and Kevin Anderson and am passionate about building a sustainable future, now I would say a liveable one, for all of us. I always try to be concientious and committed about everything that I do. In my involvement with this I am keen to contribute my considerable experience of administration and management of contractors as well as assisting with practical works both technical and general labouring.
