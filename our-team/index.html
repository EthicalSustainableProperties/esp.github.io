Our Team

Our Team

  • Larch

    Dr. Larch Maxey LLB, MSc, Ph.D., FRGS. Has researched, written about & co-created pioneering sustainable communities since 1994, including Co-founding Lammas, the UK's first Low Impact Community to get prospective planning permission; national charity the Network of Wellbeing & 2 internationally renowned research groups. Dr. Maxey initiated Wales' One Planet Development Policy, was chair of the Ecological Land Cooperative & Director of Bright Green Futures, the community self-build enterprise responsible for Bristol's most sustainable homes. Larch has co-edited 2 books & is devoted father & Short Moments practitioner, enthusiastic surfer & passionate gardener.

  • Angus

    Hi, I'm Angus, originally from South Africa with over 30 years of experience as a software engineer. I went on a hunger strike for 37 days outside the Houses of Parliament, successfully securing the UK’s first-ever briefing of MPs on climate science. As a seasoned campaigner, I’m passionate about building a sustainable future. I set up the website, organise meetings, and help keep things running smoothly. It’s inspiring to be part of a team working toward affordable, sustainable homes and making a real difference

  • Tony

    I'm Tony, living in Scotland but originally from London and often there, a retired telecommunications and electrical engineer. I am a seasoned climate campaigner and have been concerned about the climate and environment since I was a child. I am a keen student of the work of climate scientists Bill McGuire, Peter Kalmus, Peter Carter and Kevin Anderson and am passionate about building a sustainable future, now I would say a liveable one, for all of us. I always try to be concientious and committed about everything that I do. In my involvement with this I am keen to contribute my considerable experience of administration and management of contractors as well as assisting with practical works both technical and general labouring.
